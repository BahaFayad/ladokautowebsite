FAQ

Q: What service does Ladok Auto provide?

A: Ladok Auto provides its users free access to a service of reminders and auto-registration from courses and exams that are in Ladok.

Q: Is Ladok Auto secure?

A: Yes. Ladok Auto is designed with high standards for data security. Your password and username is encrypted on our server to keep your account safe. It is worth mentioning that no service can fully guarantee absolute security. If you suspect anything malicious is happening, you can always delete your Ladok Auto account from the settings menu and change your MDU password.

Q: Is Ladok Auto part of Ladok?

A: No. Ladok Auto is an independent, non-profitable organizationwith a goal of making the study period less stressful for students.

Q: Why doesn't Ladok have an official way of auto-registering or sending reminders for courses?

A: We don't know either which is why Ladok Auto was decided to be developed.

Q: Why does Ladok Auto use discord as a reminders platform?

A: We use discord for reminders because of its wide user-base and to be able to keep the service free of charge. Discord doesn't charge any fees for servers and direct messages. An SMS or e-mail service would have significantly increased the upkeep cost of Ladok Auto, making it unsustainable to upkeep without it affecting our users.

Q: What is the history of Ladok Auto and how did it emerge?

A: It all began when two students from Mälardalen University in Västerås was feeling high stress because of Ladoks awful registration system. At one instance, one of the students even missed an examination because of Ladoks system. This is when Ladok Auto was beginning to be developed (Early 2026).
